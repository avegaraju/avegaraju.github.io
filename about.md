## My Journey

I am in the software development field since 2005. I have worked in many domains starting from finance to security platforms to supply chain and e-commerce domains. I am a hands-on developer and an effective development team lead with an inclination towards technical architecture.

I have contributed to the architecture of some of the world's topmost high traffic services. My experience spans across various technologies and methodologies, always focusing on creating robust, scalable solutions that solve real-world problems.

## What I Do

- **Backend Development**: Designing and implementing scalable web services and APIs
- **Cloud Architecture**: Building cloud-native applications on AWS and other platforms  
- **Technical Leadership**: Mentoring teams and establishing best practices
- **Domain Expertise**: E-commerce, supply chain, finance, and security platforms
